Chai ETC

Needs a software in python (use MySQL) that does the following -

Register
Leads 
Supplier
Employee
Machines
Parts
Items (Raw-Material)
Bank

Leads are registered
Demo is scheduled 
Employee is assigned to each schedule
Demo feedback is sent by email & followed.

Leads convert to Clients
Work order is received
Agreement is signed
Clients locations are assigned Machines
Machine delivery and testing is done.
Client acceptance is confirmed by OTP for each installation.

Machines consume items and produce Billable-items
At least minimum Billable-items are invoiced with GST(SGST/CGST/IGST) to clients on monthly basis
Clients are also invoiced for breakage 
Billable-items are also scrapped 
Payments are received from clients against invoices.
TDS will be deducted by client against each payment.
Traces confirmation for each tds deducted is to be done.
Debit notes can be received against invoices

Plan of purchase is generated against each machine
Items and parts are purchased from suppliers against PO
Supplier give items and/or parts
Items and parts are received against PO
Supplier invoices are received against PO
Payments are made against Client invoices
TDS will be deducted against each payment.
Credit notes can be released against Supplier Invoices
TDS certificates are sent to Suppliers 

Plan of service is generated against each machine
Complaints/Tickets are registered against each Machine
Technician is assigned for each service/ticket

Parts are required to service/repair a product
